Melbourne International Undergraduate Scholarship (MIUS)

The Melbourne International Undergraduate Scholarship (MIUS) is one of Australia's most accessible undergraduate scholarships — you don't need to apply separately. All eligible international students who receive an unconditional offer of admission to an undergraduate degree at the University of Melbourne are automatically considered.

Overview

The MIUS is awarded in three tiers based on academic achievement: 25%, 50%, or 100% tuition fee reduction. The scholarship is automatically applied when you receive your admission offer — there is no additional application form to submit. This makes it one of the easiest large-value scholarships to access, requiring only that you maintain the academic standard needed to receive an admission offer.

What does the scholarship cover?

  • 25% tuition fee reduction (Tier 1 — for strong applicants)
  • 50% tuition fee reduction (Tier 2 — for high-achieving applicants)
  • 100% tuition fee reduction (Tier 3 — for exceptional applicants)
  • Awarded for the full published duration of your undergraduate degree
  • No separate application required — automatic consideration with admission

Eligibility requirements

You must be an international student commencing (not continuing) an undergraduate degree at the University of Melbourne. You must receive an unconditional offer of admission. The scholarship is available to students from selected priority countries — check the current list on the Melbourne scholarships website. You must not have previously studied at an Australian university at undergraduate level. Specific combined degrees and some dual-degree programs may be excluded.

Important — check official requirements

Scholarship conditions, values, and deadlines change annually. Always verify the current requirements on the official provider website before applying. Use the "Apply officially" button above to access the authoritative source.

How to apply

Apply for admission to an undergraduate degree at the University of Melbourne through the standard admissions process. Once you receive an unconditional offer, you will be automatically assessed for the MIUS. No separate scholarship application is required. Your scholarship offer (if successful) will be included in your admission offer letter. The key deadlines are your admission application deadlines: 31 May for Semester 2 (July) start, and 31 October for Semester 1 (February/March) start.
