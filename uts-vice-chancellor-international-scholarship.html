UTS Vice-Chancellor's International Postgraduate Scholarship

The UTS Vice-Chancellor's International Postgraduate Scholarship is one of the most generous coursework Master's scholarships available at an Australian university, covering the full cost of tuition for the duration of the program. UTS is a world top-200 university, particularly strong in technology, business, design, and health disciplines.

Provider
University of Technology Sydney (UTS)

What does the scholarship cover?

  • Full tuition fee waiver for the standard duration of the program
  • Access to UTS's industry-connected research and teaching facilities
  • No bond or return-home requirement after graduation
  • Eligible for post-study work rights (Temporary Graduate visa) after graduation

Frequently asked questions

Does this scholarship cover living expenses?+
No — the scholarship covers tuition only. You will need to budget separately for living expenses (approximately AUD 22,000–29,000 per year in Sydney).
Which programs are eligible?+
The scholarship is available for selected postgraduate coursework programs. Check the UTS Scholarships page for the current list of eligible programs, as this varies by intake.
How competitive is this scholarship?+
Very competitive — UTS awards a limited number of these each year to the highest-achieving applicants. A strong academic record (typically 75%+ in your bachelor's) and a well-prepared application are essential.
Can I work while studying at UTS?+
Yes — international students on a Subclass 500 visa can work up to 48 hours per fortnight during term, and unlimited hours during scheduled breaks.
Do I need to apply separately?+
In most cases, you apply for the scholarship through the UTS application portal alongside your course application. Check the UTS Scholarships page for the current application process.
