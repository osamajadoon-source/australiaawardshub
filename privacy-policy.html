1. Who we are

Australia Awards Hub ("we", "us", "our") operates the website australiaawardshub.com — a free, independent scholarship information resource for international students considering study in Australia. We are not affiliated with the Australian Government, any Australian university, or any scholarship provider listed on this site.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us through our contact form.

2. What information we collect

Information you provide to us

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect the following information that you choose to provide:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • WhatsApp or phone number (optional)
  • Country of citizenship
  • Education level and intended study level
  • Field of study
  • Target intake year
  • English language test status
  • Topic of enquiry
  • Your message
  • How you found us (optional)

You are not required to provide any of this information to browse the website. The contact form is entirely optional.

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical information through Google Analytics, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Your approximate geographic location (country/city level — we do not collect your precise location)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • IP address (anonymised)

This data is aggregated and anonymised. We cannot identify individual visitors from this information.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries — contact form submissions are used solely to respond to your question or provide the guidance you requested.
  • To improve the website — analytics data helps us understand which pages are most useful and what content to add.
  • To display relevant advertising — through Google AdSense (see Section 5).

We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails. If you contact us, we will only use your email address to respond to your specific enquiry.

4. Cookies and tracking

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — for the following purposes:

Essential cookies

These are required for the website to function correctly. They do not collect personal information and cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymised usage data. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising cookies (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites on the internet. See Section 5 for more details.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

5. Google AdSense and advertising

Australia Awards Hub uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense is operated by Google LLC. When you visit our site, Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you personalised advertisements based on your browsing history and interests.

Key things to know about AdSense on our site:

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads on our site
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings
  • You can also opt out via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page
  • We do not control what ads Google displays — ads are determined by Google's algorithms based on content relevance and your browsing history

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, visit: How Google uses data from sites that use Google services.

6. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate this website. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions. Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — website analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — website hosting and security. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography. Fonts are loaded from Google's servers. Google Privacy Policy

All external links on this site (including links to scholarship providers and universities) lead to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to read their privacy policies.

7. Data retention

Contact form submissions are received via Formspree and forwarded to our email inbox. We retain these emails for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry — typically no longer than 12 months. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months by default, in line with Google's standard data retention settings.

8. Your rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your personal data
  • Right to object — you can object to certain types of processing
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please use our contact form. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Please use our contact form — we respond within 24–48 hours on business days.
