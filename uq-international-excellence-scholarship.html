UQ International Excellence Scholarship

The UQ International Excellence Scholarship offers a 25% reduction in international tuition fees at the University of Queensland — one of Australia's most respected research universities. No separate application is required; you are automatically considered when you apply for admission.

Overview

The University of Queensland is ranked in the global top 50 (QS 2025) and is a member of Australia's prestigious Group of Eight research universities. UQ is located in Brisbane and is particularly strong in mining engineering, agriculture, life sciences, business, and law. Brisbane's lower cost of living compared to Sydney and Melbourne makes UQ an attractive option for internationally mobile students.

What does the scholarship cover?

  • 25% reduction in international tuition fees for the full program duration
  • Automatic consideration — no separate application required
  • Available across undergraduate and postgraduate coursework programs
  • Renewable subject to satisfactory academic progress at UQ

Eligibility requirements

Open to all international students commencing an eligible undergraduate or postgraduate coursework degree at UQ. Academic merit is the primary criterion. You are automatically assessed when you submit your UQ admission application — no additional scholarship form is required.

Important — check official requirements

Scholarship conditions, values, and deadlines change annually. Always verify the current requirements on the official provider website before applying. Use the "Apply officially" button above to access the authoritative source.

How to apply

Apply for admission to your chosen UQ program through the UQ admissions portal. The Excellence Scholarship is automatically assessed as part of your application. If you are successful, the scholarship will be noted in your offer letter. Ensure your application presents your academic achievements clearly.
