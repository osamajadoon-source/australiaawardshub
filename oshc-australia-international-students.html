The basics

What is OSHC and what does it cover?

Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) is private health insurance specifically designed for international students studying in Australia on a Subclass 500 student visa. It is a legal requirement set by the Australian Government under the visa condition 8501.

OSHC helps cover the cost of medical care you may need while living in Australia — including visits to a doctor, hospital treatment, emergency ambulance, and prescription medicines. Without it, a single hospital visit in Australia could cost you thousands of dollars.

What OSHC covers (all providers)

Hospital treatment (public and private hospitals) · GP (general practitioner) consultations · Specialist consultations · Prescribed medicines (up to annual cap) · Emergency ambulance · Some dental treatment (emergency) · Mental health services

What OSHC does not cover

Routine dental checkups and orthodontics · Optical (glasses and contact lenses) · Pre-existing conditions (waiting periods apply) · Cosmetic procedures · Pregnancy and maternity (for most providers, 12-month waiting period applies)

Step by step

How to buy OSHC — before your visa application

OSHC must be purchased before you apply for your student visa. Here is the exact process:

1

Know your course duration

Your OSHC must cover your entire course — from your course start date to your course end date. If your course runs for 2 years, you need 2 years of OSHC. Check your Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) for exact dates.

2

Choose your provider and get a quote

Visit the provider websites above and enter your course dates to get an exact quote. You do not need to use the provider your university suggests — you can choose any government-approved provider.

3

Purchase online and receive your certificate

Pay online with a credit or debit card. You will receive your OSHC certificate (Membership Certificate or Certificate of Insurance) by email within minutes. Save this document — you will need it for your visa application.

4

Include your OSHC certificate in your visa application

Upload your OSHC certificate when completing your Subclass 500 student visa application on the ImmiAccount portal. Your application will not be approved without valid OSHC.

5

Keep your OSHC active for your whole stay

Do not let your OSHC lapse while you are in Australia. If your course is extended, you must extend your OSHC to match. Contact your provider to extend online — it takes minutes.

Can I buy OSHC through my university?

Yes — many universities offer OSHC as part of your enrolment. However, you are not required to use your university's provider. You can buy from any of the four providers above, often at the same or lower price. Always compare before paying through your university portal.
