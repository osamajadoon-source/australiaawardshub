Destination Australia Scholarships

The Australian Government's Destination Australia program offers AUD $15,000 per year to support both Australian and international students studying at regional campuses — one of the few government scholarships open to both cohorts.

Overview

The Destination Australia program is a flagship Australian Government initiative designed to attract students — both domestic and international — to study and live in regional Australia. It recognises that regional universities and campuses offer world-class education while providing students with a unique, community-focused experience.

With over 1,000 scholarships available per year, this is one of the most accessible government scholarship programs in Australia. Participating institutions include a broad network of regional campuses across Queensland, New South Wales, Victoria, South Australia, Western Australia, Tasmania, and the Northern Territory.

What does the scholarship cover?

  • AUD $15,000 per year toward living and study expenses
  • Available for Certificate IV, Diploma, Bachelor, Graduate Certificate, Graduate Diploma, Masters, and Doctorate qualifications
  • Study must be at a regional campus of an eligible Australian university or TAFE
  • Open to both domestic Australian students and international students
  • Can be combined with other scholarships (check conditions with your provider)

Eligibility requirements

To be eligible for a Destination Australia Scholarship, you must:

  • Be enrolling or enrolled at a regional campus of a participating institution
  • Be studying at Certificate IV level or above
  • Meet your institution's individual academic and English language requirements
  • International students must hold a valid student visa (Subclass 500)
  • Not be enrolled in an offshore (distance/online only) mode of study
Important — check official requirements. Scholarship conditions, values, and deadlines change annually. Always verify the current requirements on the official provider website before applying. Participating institutions manage their own selection process.

How to apply

Destination Australia scholarships are administered by individual participating institutions, not centrally. The process is:

  • Identify a participating regional campus and program you wish to study
  • Apply for admission to that institution and program
  • Apply for the Destination Australia scholarship through your institution's scholarship portal — this is a separate process from admission
  • Selection criteria vary by institution but typically include academic merit, personal statement, and demonstrated commitment to studying regionally

Visit the Australian Government's Destination Australia page for a current list of participating institutions and their direct scholarship links.
