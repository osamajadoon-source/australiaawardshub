IELTS Requirements for Australia

Complete guide to scores, preparation, and requirements for scholarships & visas.

Why You Need IELTS for Australia

Whether applying for Australia Awards, university scholarships, or a Subclass 500 Student Visa, you must prove English proficiency. IELTS Academic is the most widely accepted test.

✅ Accepted By

  • All Australian Universities (Group of 8 & others)
  • Australia Awards (DFAT)
  • Department of Home Affairs

Test Type

For scholarships and university admission, you need IELTS Academic, not General Training.

IELTS for Student Visa (Subclass 500)

The Department of Home Affairs requires English evidence for visas.

  • Bachelor/Master/PhD: Minimum IELTS 6.0 (with no band less than 5.5)
  • Diploma: Minimum IELTS 5.5

Note: Your university's requirement is usually higher than the visa minimum.

Test Format & Booking

📖 Listening (30 mins)

4 recordings, 40 questions.

📖 Reading (60 mins)

3 long texts, 40 questions.

✍️ Writing (60 mins)

Task 1 (Chart/Graph) + Task 2 (Essay).

🗣️ Speaking (11-14 mins)

Face-to-face interview with examiner.

Booking: Tests are held up to 4 times a month. Cost is approx AUD $395-$450 depending on country. Results take 13 days.

Preparation Tips

  • Practice Tests: Use Cambridge IELTS books (10-18) for authentic practice.
  • Vocabulary: Learn academic word lists (AWL).
  • Writing Feedback: Get your essays reviewed by a tutor; self-study isn't enough for Writing.
  • Speaking: Record yourself and compare with band 9 examples on YouTube.
