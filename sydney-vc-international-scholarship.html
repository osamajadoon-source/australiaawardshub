University of Sydney Vice-Chancellor's International Scholarship (VCIS)

The University of Sydney Vice-Chancellor's International Scholarship (VCIS) is an automatic merit scholarship awarded to high-achieving international students at the time of their admission offer. No separate application is required. The scholarship reduces tuition fees by AUD 5,000 to AUD 40,000 per year depending on your academic achievement.

Overview

The University of Sydney is Australia's oldest and most prestigious university, ranked in the global top 20–50 across multiple QS subject rankings. The VCIS is designed to attract and reward talented international students across all faculties. Because it requires no separate application, it is one of the easiest merit scholarships to access.

What does the scholarship cover?

  • AUD 5,000 to AUD 40,000 per year reduction in tuition fees
  • Automatically applied — no separate application required
  • Available across undergraduate and postgraduate coursework programs
  • Renewable annually subject to satisfactory academic progress at Sydney

Eligibility requirements

You must be an international student receiving an offer of admission to an eligible undergraduate or postgraduate coursework degree at the University of Sydney. The scholarship amount is determined by your academic results at the time of application. Higher academic achievement results in a higher scholarship tier. Not all programs are eligible — the scholarship is primarily available for on-campus degrees.

Important — check official requirements

Scholarship conditions, values, and deadlines change annually. Always verify the current requirements on the official provider website before applying. Use the "Apply officially" button above to access the authoritative source.

How to apply

Apply for admission to your chosen degree at the University of Sydney through the standard admissions portal. Your scholarship will be assessed automatically and included in your admission offer letter if you qualify. No additional form or application is required.
