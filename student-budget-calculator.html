Part-time work tip

International students can work up to 48 hours per fortnight during term and unlimited hours during university breaks. At minimum wage (AUD $24.10/hr), 48 hours per fortnight = approximately $578/month — which can significantly offset your living costs.

What's included in your OSHC — don't pay twice

Your OSHC covers GP visits, hospital stays, emergency ambulance, and some prescription medicines. You don't need to budget separately for basic medical care if you hold OSHC — but optical, dental checkups, and physiotherapy are extras not covered by standard OSHC.
