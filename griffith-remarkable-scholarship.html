Griffith Remarkable Scholarship

The Griffith Remarkable Scholarship offers exceptional international students a 50% tuition fee reduction for undergraduate and postgraduate coursework study at Griffith University — one of Queensland's largest and most diverse universities.

Overview

Griffith University is a public research university with campuses across South East Queensland — Gold Coast, Brisbane (Nathan and Mt Gravatt), and Logan. It ranks in the global top 2% of universities and is particularly strong in music, environmental science, criminology, and health sciences.

The Griffith Remarkable Scholarship (formerly part of the Vice-Chancellor's scholarship suite) is one of the university's most generous merit scholarships for international students. At 50% of tuition fees, it can represent a saving of AUD $15,000–$20,000+ over the duration of a degree, depending on the program.

What does the scholarship cover?

  • 50% reduction in tuition fees for the full duration of the enrolled program
  • Deducted automatically each trimester/semester from your tuition invoice
  • Available for both undergraduate and postgraduate coursework degrees
  • Applicable at all Griffith campuses (Gold Coast, Nathan, Mt Gravatt, Logan)

Eligibility requirements

To be eligible for the Griffith Remarkable Scholarship, you must:

  • Be an international student (not an Australian citizen or permanent resident)
  • Be applying for or commencing an eligible undergraduate or postgraduate coursework degree
  • Demonstrate exceptional academic achievement — typically in the top 5–10% of your graduating cohort
  • Meet Griffith University's English language requirements for your chosen program
  • Be a new, commencing student (not a continuing or transferring student)
Important — check official requirements. Scholarship conditions, values, and deadlines change each year. Always verify the current eligibility criteria and application process on the official Griffith University scholarships website before applying.

How to apply

The Griffith Remarkable Scholarship is applied for through Griffith University's scholarship portal:

  • Apply for admission to Griffith University first via their online application system
  • Submit a separate scholarship application through the Griffith scholarships portal
  • Provide academic transcripts demonstrating exceptional results
  • Some applications may require a personal statement outlining your academic achievements and goals

Griffith University operates on a trimester system with intakes in March, July, and November. Scholarship deadlines are typically aligned with each intake — check the Griffith scholarships page for specific dates.
