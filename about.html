About us

We have been where you are right now.

Australia Awards Hub is built by a team of scholarship alumni who have studied at universities across Australia — including Australia Awards scholars, Research Training Program (RTP) recipients, and university merit scholarship holders. We built this because we wished it existed when we were applying.

The Australia Awards Scholarship application is one of the most demanding scholarship processes in the world. It asks for a detailed personal statement, a clear articulation of your development goals, referee reports, English test results, and evidence of professional experience — all submitted through a government portal with a hard 2pm AEST deadline.

Most students who are genuinely qualified don't make it past the first round. Not because they aren't good enough — but because they applied to the wrong scholarship, started too late, wrote a generic personal statement, or didn't understand what the selection panel was actually looking for.

"We didn't build this site to aggregate information. We built it because we know exactly what it feels like to sit in front of that OASIS form at 1am, not knowing if your application is strong enough."

Our team includes Australia Awards scholars who have studied at the University of Sydney, University of Melbourne, ANU, and Monash University. We also include holders of RTP stipend scholarships, university merit scholarships, and postgraduate research packages — across fields including civil engineering, public health, education, environmental science, and public policy.

We have lived the experience — the IELTS preparation, the visa application, the GTE statement, the arrival in a new city, the first semester, the part-time work, and eventually the graduation. Every guide on this site is written from that firsthand experience, not from a textbook or a government website.
